Big week for running backs, making the group question that second flex spot. Enough of an intro, let’s get to it.

  • Zac’s team is more up and down then the stock market. Well after this week he’s got $10 more dollars to invest. Just take that $10 from your Venmo account right to your Barstool Sportsbook account, put in a college gameday parlay….

  • So far Petty is leading all teams with the most points for. Unfortunately, sits at 2-3 overall. Lowest scoring game is only 123, could be an early sleeper candidate come playoffs.

  • Let’s Get Fiscal, Commisionary Position and Herbie Hanc0ck all sitting at the top with a record of 4-1. Fiscal looking the strongest with 705 points for. Could this be the year for ol pocket balls? Getting solid production out of draft steals Nick Chubb (4th) and Lockett (drafted in the 9th…and dropped by Smith, yikes)

However, he chalked up another L after playing Conor who needed a win after some bad beats himself. Name has officially been changed to Suicide Squad. Someone put 988 on speed dial.

Yes, against all odds, Kevin’s team is still trending down.
