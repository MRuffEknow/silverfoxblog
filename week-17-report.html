Season Recap

Week 17 Recap - Playoffs Final Round

  • Congrats to Ryan on his first championship win. His first championship game and his team came out swinging with a whopping 161 points. Enough to beat any team that week. Looking forward to your decision on next year's punishment.

  • Conor finished in 3rd place but what a dud. Pretty remarkable that a team could come out with a 3rd place finish by only putting up 90 points. Any respectable owner would take his winnings and donate them to charity.

  • Every year there has to be a loser, and unfortunately for Petty once again that is him. Just a bad beat of a season really. Team barely missed playoffs ending with a 7-7 record. Not only that, but finished in 3rd for points for! That's the cruel game of fantasy for you; sometimes it's better to be lucky than good. Excited to see the girl scout cookie sale this summer.

Teams Awards

Horse Shoe In the Ass Award * David (Fight For 2nd O Last): Ended the regular season with the most dogs of the week and only 2 wins. But won when it counted.

Dog of the Year Award * Ben (Chin Diapers): Takes the award for putting up a measly 50 points in Week 17

Waiver Wire Whore Award * Zac (Mind Goblin): Finished with 45 acquisitions, 14 higher than the next highest owner. This guy gets no sleep Tuesday nights.

BDOW Award * Kellen (Jeffersionian Republic): 192 points in week 15. Barely missed playoffs but a strong win to start the consolation ladder.
