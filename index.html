Week 12 Recap - I can feel in my plums

Only two weeks left in the regular season! The final 6 playoff spots are very much up for grabs as only 2 teams are mathematically out (Note: did not do the math). Currently Petty and Kellen are on the outside looking in; big upsets since both sit at 2 and 5 in the standings for most points for. 4 teams sit at 7-5 (Zac, Conor, Ben and Commish) which means the next two weeks are crucial. There's a possibility that Zac, sitting at #1 for Points For ends up in the loser's bracket slinging thin mints. Going to be a fun two weeks, no time to hold back, start your fliers, it's going to get fun.

  • Zac's 4th Big Dick of the Week, most of anyone so far

  • Kellen with one of the worst bad beats of the season. 149 points was the 3rd highest of the weekend. Forces him out of the playoff picture with two weeks to go

  • Petty with a huge win still fighting for a playoff spot

  • Some major butt clenches of the week. Wins from Justin, Ben and Justin keep them in the playoff hunt

  • Hard fought win by Kevin to take Dog of the Week, surprisingly his first of the year

  • Lastly, Ben needs to post his fucking combine video or I'm removing him from playoffs
