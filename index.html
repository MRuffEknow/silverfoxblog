Week 8 Recap

Some big scoing this week led to one of the higher point totals for teams across the board. Coming down the home stretch it's going to be a tight race for the final 6 playoff teams.

  • Kellen with his second BDoW honors. Massive games from AJ Brown and Kamara made this team unbeatable this week.

  • Ben pulled off a much needed win riding Pollard harder then Jerry Jones himself. Not sure if it's sustainable with Zeke coming back though.

  • Not the Halloween Conor needed out of his homeboy Joe Burrow. Guy asked for a treat and instead got a trick. That brings Petty, Kellen and Conor all to 4-4.
